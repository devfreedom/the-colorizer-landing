We are The Colorizer

The Colorizer is a global sociocultural tourism platform. Ranging from ancient history to pop culture, reaching from Far East to Carribean Sea, we aim to promote your international cultural tourism by sharing informative, educational, yet entertaining tidbits of the world. With The Colorizer, you can color your horizon.

Glocalizing your tourism

We spread your local festival to the world, then we deliver overseas museum exhibitions to your room. We connect local and foreign cultures by showcasing a cultural mosaic of the globe. See outside the box with us. Visit and find out by yourself.

Focus on human stories

One of our primary interests is people, someone like you, someone like us. Because human matters. Instead of showing buildings and artifacts in silence, we want to share people's stories behind. We also ask you questions to get to know more about you and your world. Color us with your own stories.

In the spirit of inclusion

The Colorizer spotlights lesser-known cultural places and things that deserve more attention. Because we want to emphasize the unique beauty of every culture on earth. When tourism meets curiosity, a colorful parade appears.

In the end, goodwill
